设计思维

Design Thinking

The real world is uncertainty. We need creative idea to resolve the problem. This is not because the old method not working. The people like the new stuff.

Empathize

The importance of empathy, you are the best to resolve your own problem.
you don’t understand the pain point of other people.
How to empathize?

  • listen - make friend with your customer.
  • engage
  • observe - dig deeper, let them tell the story.

Define

Use the story you gathered from previous stage
Come up with a problem statement.
Not creating thing the user want but what they need.

ideate

come up with many ideas, not one perfect idea
Try brainstorming

  • go for quantity
  • go for wild idea
  • defer judgement
  • build on the idea of others
  • stay focus on the topic
  • be visual
  • one conversation at a time
  • make a diverse team
    wrap up: don’t just walk away from your post it board.
  1. cluster similar idea together(different effort, different impact)
  2. Organize your idea
  3. Priority ideas
  4. Commit to next steps

prototype

  • To show your concept
  • Bare minimum
  • Lowest cost
  • Be disposable
  • Fail fast, fail Cheap
    科大讯飞，真人活人同声传译

Test

Test as early as possible
Test with real user
Don’t defend your idea
Get feedback(控制论，反馈)
Go back to step 1（调整）

The Anti-pattern

Don’t use design thinking all the time
Design thinking is a method not a magi
